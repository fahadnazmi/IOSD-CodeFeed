Stats About COVID-19

(as of 15 February, 2021)

How it Started India

India witnessed an outbreak of the coronavirus, otherwise known as COVID-19, or SARS-CoV-2 in late January 2020 when three Indian students travelled to the southern state of Kerala from Wuhan in China - the epicenter of the outbreak.

Situation Around the Globe

Number of cases worldwide as of February 15, 2021 are 109,413,396.
Country with the highest cases is United States.
Country with the highest per million testing among most impacted countries is Israel.

Situation in India

Total number of confirmed cases are 10,925,710. The state with most cases and deaths is Maharashtra.
